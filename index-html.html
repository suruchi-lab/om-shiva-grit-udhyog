About Us

Delivering Quality and Reliability Since 2005

Your Trusted Partner in Construction Materials

Om Shiva Grit Udhyog is a family-owned business established in 2005, specializing in premium stone crushing and aggregates production. Located in the mineral-rich region of Rajasthan, we leverage the finest natural resources to deliver superior quality products for construction and infrastructure projects.

With state-of-the-art machinery and a dedicated team, we ensure consistent quality in every batch. Our commitment to excellence has made us a preferred supplier for major construction companies across Rajasthan and neighboring states.

Why Choose Us?

  • Premium quality stone aggregates with perfect gradation
  • Consistent supply capability for projects of any scale
  • Stringent quality control and testing protocols
  • Environment-friendly operational practices
  • Prompt delivery and excellent customer service
